Alternative Housing Options-Off Campus

Tennessee State University does not require its students to live on campus at any point of their academic career. In recent years, the University has consistently reached capacity of its on-campus facilities, forcing students who have delayed in submitting their housing application to reside elsewhere. If you find yourself applying late or wish to find off-campus accommodation, feel free to visit the following websites for alternative housing options:
