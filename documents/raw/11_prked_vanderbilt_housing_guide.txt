It’s a lottery system, &amp; seniority plays a big role. Current off-campus residents &amp; rising fourth-years get priority.</div><div class="MuiTypography-root MuiTypography-body1  mui-7ja7xn">So, the big takeaway here is: <strong>DO NOT SIGN A LEASE</strong> until you have that official, in-writing authorization from Vanderbilt. It’s nestled right between Vandy &amp; Belmont University, so the area is buzzing with student energy.</div><ul><li><strong>The Vibe:</strong> Think cozy coffee shops, cute boutiques, &amp; a lively, youthful atmosphere. There&#x27;s a historic, non-profit movie theater, the Belcourt, which is perfect for a low-key night out.</li><li><strong>Who Lives Here:</strong> It&#x27;s a mix of undergrads, grad students, &amp; young professionals. It&#x27;s also incredibly walkable &amp; borders Centennial Park, a huge green space with the iconic Parthenon replica.</li><li><strong>Who Lives Here:</strong> You&#x27;ll find a lot of students &amp; young professionals here, drawn by the proximity to campus, the nightlife, &amp; the slightly more affordable rent compared to some other nearby areas.</li><li><strong>The Downside:</strong> The transit options aren&#x27;t as robust as in some other neighborhoods, so you might want a car if you live here. It’s a bustling area with a high concentration of bars, restaurants, &amp; apartment buildings. It&#x27;s a bit more of a &quot;grown-up&quot; a nightlife scene than Elliston Place, but still very popular with students.</div><ul><li><strong>The Vibe:</strong> Midtown is energetic &amp; fast-paced.
