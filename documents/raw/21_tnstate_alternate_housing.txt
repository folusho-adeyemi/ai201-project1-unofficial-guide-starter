            <p>
               <strong>Alternative Housing Options-Off Campus</strong>
            </p>
            <p>
               <span>
                  <em>Tennessee State University</em> does not require its students to live on campus at any point of their academic career. In recent years, the University has consistently reached capacity of its on-campus facilities, forcing students who have delayed in submitting their housing application to reside elsewhere.  </span>
               <span>If you find yourself applying late or wish to find off-campus accommodation, feel free to visit the following websites for alternative housing options:</span>
            </p>
            <p>
               <span>
                  <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Apartment Indexes:</span> </span>
            </p>
            <ul>
               <li>
                  <a title="Trulia " href="http://www.trulia.com">
                     <span>www.trulia.com</span>
                  </a>
               </li>
               <li>
                  <a href="http://www.zillow.com">
                     <span>www.zillow.com</span>
                  </a>
               </li>
               <li>
                  <a href="http://www.rent.com/tennessee/tennessee-state-university-apartments">www.rent.com</a>
               </li>
               <li>
                  <span>
                     <a href="http://www.forrent.com/">www.forrent.com</a>
                  </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                  <span>
                     <a href="http://www.apartmentfinder.com/">www.apartmentfinder.com</a>
                  </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                  <a href="https://www.apartmentlist.com/tn/nashville">www.apartmentlist.com/tn/nashville</a> </li>
            </ul>
            <p> <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Airbnb, Flipkey and Vacation Rentals </span>offer monthly rentals/sublets for competitive prices</p>
            <ul>
               <li>
                  <a href="https://www.airbnb.com">https://www.airbnb.com</a>
               </li>
               <li>
                  <a href="https://flipkey.com">https://flipkey.com</a>
               </li>
               <li>
                  <a href="https://vrbo.com">https://vrbo.com</a>
               </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
               <strong>
                  <br/>
               </strong>
               <em>Please utilize the following zip codes for available leases and rentals closest to the TSU Campus</em>:<br/>The zip code 37209 for TSU Main Campus<br/>The zip code 37203 for TSU’s Avon Williams Off-Campus Instructional Site.<strong>
                  <br/>
                  <br/>
                  <br/>Short Term Accommodation</strong>
            </p>
            <p>
               <span>
                  <span>For Emergency Housing, please refer to the <a title="Metropolitan Development and Housing Agency"
                         href="https://www.nashville-mdha.org/community-development-department/homeless-assistance/">Metropolitan Development and Housing Agency website</a>
                  </span>
               </span>
            </p>
            <p>For Short-Term Accommodation, please refer to this list of <a title="Area Hotels" href="/orientation/List%20of%20Hotels.pdf">Area Hotels</a>. Please inquire with the hotel(s) directly for additional information. </p>
